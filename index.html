Who Are We?

Riddlebit Software is a game development company based in Trondheim, Norway. We're a small team of developers and artists that share a passion for making games. Currently we're making an awesome game called JETRUNNER.

Follow Us on Social Media

Current Project: JETRUNNER

A fast-paced parkour-driven first-person shooter. JETRUNNER is brought to life with striking cel-shaded, comic-like visuals. The core of its design is a gameplay loop that blends the high-speed optimization of Trackmania with the expressive movement of Titanfall. This creates a title focused on delivering rich, fluid movement mechanics alongside precise, satisfying gameplay, designed for both player enjoyment and competitive viability.
